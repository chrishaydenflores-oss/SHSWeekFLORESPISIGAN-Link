About LCBA

LCBA, or Laguna College of Business and Arts, is a prestigious institution located in Calamba City, Philippines. It offers a wide range of academic programs, including the Senior High School (SHS) curriculum with various strands. The school is known for its commitment to providing quality education and fostering a supportive learning environment for its students.

Information & Communications Technology

The ICT strand at LCBA prepares students for careers in the technology sector, covering topics such as computer hardware, software development, networking, and digital communication. Students gain hands-on experience with the latest technologies and learn how to apply their skills in real-world scenarios, making them well-equipped for future opportunities in the rapidly evolving field of information and communications technology.

Hardware: Computers, servers, and smartphones. Networking: Internet, wireless systems, and telecommunications. Software: Applications, cloud computing, and data tools.
